Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

TapTrap ("we", "our", "the app") is developed by MNPekdemir. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

TapTrap is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Session Data: Tap counts, timestamps, and tap positions are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone.
  • Photos: If you use Stealth Mode, your selected photo is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or access your photo library beyond your selection.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase data is handled by Apple (App Store), Google (Play Store), and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment details.
  • Advertising: For free users, we use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

All app data (sessions, settings, PIN, custom backgrounds) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and the app's document directory. We do not operate servers or databases that store your personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — for managing subscriptions and purchases. Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements to free users. Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

TapTrap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ / Everyone and does not contain inappropriate content.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all data at any time from Settings > Reset All Data, or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
